The Example of image alignmet


HTML allows for simple aligning of text and images by adding the style attributes to the tag. HTML 5 expanded this attribute by specifying float: right options. when you use these options,text flows down the right or left side of an image aligned to them. the text continues untill either the text passes the image, the text ends,or the browser encounters a tag.
